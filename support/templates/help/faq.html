  1. {% trans "My total goal is missing!!!" %}
    {% blocktrans %}

    This isn't a question. That's you complaining, but I have an answer anyway.

    You most likely had a phantom goal This isn't anything to do with Danny Phantom, ghosts or an own goal nobody wants to own up to. Instead, this related to goals shown by the Discord bot. Before the 22nd December, once you reached your goal the bot would reset it to zero. Anybody below Level 40 and a zero goal was assigned a phantom goal as a promotion for goals.

    Since then, the default behaviour is to automatically move your goal on to the next level when you reach your goal, rather than resetting to zero. To give the user a choice not to have a goal. We removed phantom goals. Phantom goals didn't congratulate the user either, whereas set goals do.

    {% endblocktrans %}
  2. {% trans "How do I set a goal?" %}
    {% url 'discord' as discord_url %} {% blocktrans %}

    Goals currently only work on the Discord bot, however, they're coming soon to web

    Simply message the Discord Bot, TrainerDex#8522 .update goal <option> <value>

    Options

    • daily - Sets your daily or 'rate' goal. This is the goal which shows you the speed at which you're progressing. This takes a numeric value ranging from 0 to 3000000.
    • total - Sets your total or 'reach' goal. This is the goal which you would use to get a grasp of when you're going to reach a specific point. This takes a numeric value which is at least one more than your current experience. Sidenote - .update goal total has an additional option, auto, which sets a goal for you based on your current experince.
    {% endblocktrans %}
